# 1.2.6 Sign Language (Prerecorded)

**Level:** AAA
**Versions:** WCAG 2.0, 2.1, 2.2
**Principle:** 1 Perceivable
**Guideline:** 1.2 Time-based Media
**Reference:** [1.2.6 Sign Language (Prerecorded)](https://www.w3.org/WAI/WCAG22/Understanding/sign-language-prerecorded.html)

## Description

Sign language interpretation is provided for all prerecorded audio content in synchronized media.

## Sufficient Techniques

- [G54](https://www.w3.org/WAI/WCAG22/Techniques/general/G54): Including a sign language interpreter in the video stream
- [G81](https://www.w3.org/WAI/WCAG22/Techniques/general/G81): Providing a synchronized video of the sign language interpreter that can be displayed in a different viewport or overlaid on the image by the player
  - [SM13](https://www.w3.org/WAI/WCAG22/Techniques/smil/SM13): Providing sign language interpretation through synchronized video streams in SMIL 1.0
  - [SM14](https://www.w3.org/WAI/WCAG22/Techniques/smil/SM14): Providing sign language interpretation through synchronized video streams in SMIL 2.0
